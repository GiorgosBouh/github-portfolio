Clinical Pilates for Women 50+: Bridging Healthcare Education with Community Wellness

Posted on January 30, 2026

At the CIDERR of Metropolitan College, we are proud to announce the launch of our specialized Clinical Pilates sessions, specifically designed for women over the age of 50. This initiative focuses on the management and improvement of musculoskeletal conditions, promoting mobility, core strength, and overall quality of life.

A Dual Mission: Excellence in Care and Education This program is more than just a fitness class; it is a vital part of our educational ecosystem. By integrating these sessions into our curriculum, we provide a unique opportunity for our students from the Faculty of Health and Life Sciences and the Faculty of Sports Sciences.

Real-World Experience: Our students engage daily with real cases, gaining invaluable hands-on experience.

Professional Training: Under expert supervision, they learn to apply clinical protocols in a real-world setting, preparing them for their future careers as healthcare and sports professionals. Community Impact: We provide high-quality, evidence-based exercise programs to a vital demographic, fostering a culture of health and active aging. Through this synergy, Metropolitan College continues to lead the way in clinical education while giving back to the community with services that make a tangible difference.
