Study Guide for the General Secretariat for Vocational Education, Training & Lifelong Learning

Posted on September 30, 2024

We are pleased to share that our team has completed the development of the study guide for the General Secretariat for Vocational Education, Training & Lifelong Learning of the Greek Ministry of Education, Religious Affairs, and Sports.

This comprehensive, modern, and up-to-date guide is tailored to support the specialty of Instructor of Free Gymnastics-Aerobics.

The guide was created with the goal of providing structured educational resources that cater to the needs of both students and instructors, enhancing the quality of training in this field.

For those who may wish to translate the guide from Greek to English, free online tools can be used to facilitate the process.
