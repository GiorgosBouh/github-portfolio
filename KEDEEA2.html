Trip Report | Visit to Athens

Posted on October 23, 2025

During my recent visit to Marousi, Athens, I had the pleasure of visiting the Center Interdisciplinary Education and Research on Rehabilitation. The visit was an inspiring opportunity to witness first-hand the innovative projects and research activities currently being carried out by the center. I had the privilege of meeting my close colleague Christos Komisopoulos and his dedicated team, who are doing truly remarkable work in advancing education, rehabilitation science, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Our discussions focused on strengthening our partnership, exchanging ideas for joint projects, and exploring future initiatives that bridge research and clinical practice. A special thanks to Christos for the warm welcome, his valuable insights, and his continued collaboration. It was a visit filled with meaningful conversations, professional inspiration, and genuine collegial spirit.
