Pregnancy, Posture, Gait, and Falls | Metropolitan College | Campus Thessaloniki

Posted on March 05, 2025

On March 4, 2025, we successfully conducted the seminar “Pregnancy, Posture, Gait, and Falls” at Metropolitan College, Thessaloniki Campus, as part of the DevelopingU initiative. The event provided valuable insights into the biomechanical changes during pregnancy, their effects on posture and gait, and the associated fall risks.

I would like to extend my sincere gratitude to the Program Leader of the Midwifery Department, Emmanouela Dimoveli, for the invitation and her support in organizing this seminar. A heartfelt thank you to all participants for their engagement and thought-provoking discussions. Your enthusiasm underscores the importance of bridging biomechanics and maternal health in clinical practice and education. Looking forward to future collaborations and knowledge-sharing opportunities!
