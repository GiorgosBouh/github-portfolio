üß† About the Project

Title: Analysis of Clinical Risk Thresholds During Pregnancy and Development of a Web-Based Decision Support Application

The application is based on statistical analysis of real-world data from the Maternal Health Risk Dataset (Ahmed et al., 2020, Springer). Importantly, it does not rely on fixed clinical rules, but rather on combinatorial statistical analysis of all relevant clinical variables, including:

  • Systolic Blood Pressure
  • Diastolic Blood Pressure
  • Blood Sugar (BS)
  • Heart Rate
  • Age

Through this multidimensional approach, the system classifies maternal risk into three levels:

  • üü• High Surveillance
  • üüß Medium Surveillance
  • üü© Low Surveillance
