Successful Completion of my 2nd Master of Science Thesis Defense'

Posted on February 05, 2025

Today, I successfully defended my Masters thesis in the MSc by Research in Intelligent Information Systems with excellent evaluation. My research, titled “Large Language Models for the Engineering of a Parkinson Disease Monitoring and Alerting Ontology,” marks the completion of a demanding yet highly rewarding academic journey.

I am deeply grateful to my supervisor, A/Professor Konstantinos Ilias Kotis, for his invaluable guidance, continuous support, and the knowledge he shared with me. His contribution was instrumental in every step of this journey. A heartfelt thank you also to the members of my thesis committee, Prof. Tsekoura and Prof. Anagnostopoulos, for their insightful feedback and constructive evaluation. This challenging but inspiring path has provided me with invaluable knowledge, research experience, and new perspectives. Excited for what comes next! Intelligent Systems Lab, University of the Aegean
