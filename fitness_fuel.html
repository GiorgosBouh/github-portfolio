Photodentro project | Fitness Fuel

Posted on June 20, 2023

Recently, I had the opportunity to collaborate with my friend and colleague, Efthimia Rova, on an interdisciplinary educational scenario, which was approved by Photodentro and is available for use by teachers of relevant specialties.

With the start of the new school year, the scenario can be utilized in the educational process, offering students an interactive and enriched learning experience.

The Fitness Fuel project, approved by Photodentro, is an interdisciplinary educational scenario designed to promote physical activity and healthy lifestyle choices through engaging, interactive learning. Targeted at students, the scenario incorporates modern technological tools and gamification to enhance the educational experience. Teachers of relevant specialties can utilize this project to inspire students to explore the principles of fitness, exercise science, and nutrition, and informatics, while fostering a deeper understanding of human physiology.
