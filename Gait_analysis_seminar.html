Hands-on Gait Analysis Workshop: From Theory to Clinical Practice

Posted on February 04, 2026

A highly successful 3-hour hands-on workshop on gait analysis was recently completed at the Center for Interdisciplinary Education and Research in Rehabilitation (CIDERR) of Metropolitan College.

The workshop brought together students and professionals from Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, and Podiatry, creating a truly interdisciplinary learning environment focused on clinical movement analysis.

As part of the hands-on experience, participants were introduced to a custom-developed gait analysis software platform developed by me, designed to support markerless motion analysis and clinical interpretation using modern computer vision techniques.

The platform is available at: https://giorgosbouh.github.io/gait_analysis_mediapipe/
