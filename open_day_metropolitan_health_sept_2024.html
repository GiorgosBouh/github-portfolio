Open Day Metropolitan College, School of Heath, Campus Thessaloniki

Posted on September 20, 2024

Today, we welcomed some of our students from the School of Health. We discussed an interdisciplinary approach to treating ischemic stroke patients and how our various departments can collaborate to improve patient health and overall quality of life.

We also introduced our upcoming Center for Interdisciplinary Education and Research in Rehabilitation, which will soon be ready and will play a key role in enhancing both the academic experience and the professional success of our students.
