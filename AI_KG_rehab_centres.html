AI + Knowledge Graph (+Ontologies) will become mandatory in modern rehabilitation centres

August 08, 2025

AI + Knowledge Graph Ontologies: The New Standard in Rehabilitation | Mandatory by 2030 — With Therapists Still Leading the Way

Why the Change Is Coming

  • ► Rehabilitation is becoming data-rich but decision-poor
  • ► Black-box AI lacks transparency and cannot meet clinical accountability standards
  • ► Regulations will soon require explainable AI, powered by Knowledge Graph (KG) ontologies

The Role of AI + KG Ontologies

Integrate biomechanics, patient history, and treatment protocols into a single reasoning system

Provide standardised terminology so all clinicians and systems “speak the same language”

Trace every recommendation back to its source data and reasoning path

AI provides evidence-backed suggestions, but final decisions stay with human therapists. Clinicians interpret AI insights in the context of patient goals, safety, and ethics. AI becomes a trusted assistant, not an autonomous decision-maker
